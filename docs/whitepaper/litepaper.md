# Ego Exchange Litepaper

## Thesis

Trading infrastructure for autonomous finance should feel as fast as a centralized exchange while remaining user-owned, permissionless, and cross-chain.

## What Ego is

Ego Exchange is a pro-grade cross-chain DEX on Lithosphere with spot, perpetuals, launch markets, AI copilots, AI agents, and a unified trader identity system.

## Why now

- traders want lower latency and lower fees
- agents need scoped permissions and deterministic budgets
- users want exposure to majors, new launches, meme markets, and onchain alpha in one venue
- projects need permissionless yet safer listing and liquidity tooling

## Core pillars

- pro terminal UX
- cross-chain routing and collateral
- AI-native workflows
- robust risk engine
- token incentives aligned with liquidity quality
- governance with parameterized safety
