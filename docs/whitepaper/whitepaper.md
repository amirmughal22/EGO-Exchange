# Ego Exchange Whitepaper

## Abstract

Ego Exchange is a permissionless, cross-chain exchange protocol for spot, perpetual, and launch-driven markets. It is designed for high-performance execution, risk-aware leverage, and bounded AI-native automation on Lithosphere.

## Architecture summary

- offchain order sequencing with onchain settlement
- modular AMM and orderbook liquidity
- cross-margin and portfolio margin
- partial-first liquidation engine
- launch pools and meme market primitives
- AI agents with signed execution receipts and bounded scopes
- staking, fee sharing, and ve-governance

## Economic model

- protocol collects trading, funding, liquidation, listing, and premium product fees
- fees are shared across treasury, stakers, insurance fund, and liquidity incentives
- emissions are directed by ve-governance toward productive liquidity and market depth
